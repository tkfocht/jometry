Construction

J!ometry is built and maintained by 2021 4-day champion and 2022 TOC qualifier John Focht. The origin story is that I realized that the published box score PDFs would be difficult to work with in the long run, and so I wanted to make sure there was a spreadsheet or CSV or something that research could be done with, once there was enough data. Then it turned out that I wanted to do some of that research and talk about it. It became useful to put it all in one stable place and semi-standardize the things I found most interesting. It also became part of my TOC prep.
